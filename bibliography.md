
# Bibliography

This page collects some of the reference sources behind [algebrica](https://algebrica.org), together with texts that are simply useful for studying mathematics. The list runs from the more elementary works to the more advanced ones, with related subjects kept close together.

+ [Basic Mathematics](https://archive.org/download/basic-mathematics-serge-lang_20240418/Basic%20Mathematics%20-%20Serge%20Lang.pdf), Serge Lang
+ [Book of Proof](https://richardhammack.github.io/BookOfProof/Main.pdf), Richard Hammack
+ [Pure Mathematics](https://archive.org/details/puremathematics0000pars), Stuart Parsonson
+ [Calculus](https://ia800204.us.archive.org/28/items/CalculusSpivak/Calculus%20-%20Spivak_text.pdf), Michael Spivak
+ [Notes on Diffy Qs](https://www.jirka.org/diffyqs/diffyqs.pdf), Jiří Lebl
+ [Advanced Calculus](https://people.math.harvard.edu/~shlomo/docs/Advanced_Calculus.pdf), Lynn H. Loomis, Shlomo Sternberg
+ [Basic Real Analysis](https://www.math.stonybrook.edu/~aknapp/download/b2-realanal-inside.pdf), Anthony W. Knapp
+ [An Introduction to Measure Theory](https://terrytao.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/gsm-126-tao5-measure-book.pdf), Terence Tao
+ [Complex Variables](https://people.math.sc.edu/girardi/m7034/book/AshComplexVariablesWithHyperlinks.pdf), Robert B. Ash
+ [Fundamentals of Matrix Algebra](https://www.apexcalculus.com/downloads/MatrixAlgebra.pdf), Gregory Hartman
+ [Linear Algebra Done Wrong](https://www.math.brown.edu/streil/papers/LADW/LADW_2017-09-04.pdf), Sergei Treil
+ [Introduction to Applied Linear Algebra](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/vmls/vmls.pdf), Stephen Boyd, Lieven Vandenberghe
+ [Matrix Calculus for Machine Learning and Beyond](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-s096-matrix-calculus-for-machine-learning-and-beyond-january-iap-2023/mit18_s096iap23_lec_full.pdf), Paige Bright, Alan Edelman, Steven G. Johnson
+ [Introduction to Probability](https://math.dartmouth.edu/~prob/prob/prob.pdf), Charles M. Grinstead, J. Laurie Snell
+ [An Introduction to Statistical Learning](https://www.statlearning.com/s/ISLR-Seventh-Printing.pdf), Gareth James, Daniela Witten, Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani
+ [The Elements of Statistical Learning](https://hastie.su.domains/Papers/ESLII.pdf), Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani, Jerome Friedman
+ [Convex Optimization](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/bv_cvxbook.pdf), Stephen Boyd, Lieven Vandenberghe
+ [A Modern Introduction to Online Learning](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1912.13213), Francesco Orabona
+ [Mathematical and Statistical Foundations of AI](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2106.11342), Saman S. Ghods
+ [Algorithms and Complexity](https://www.math.upenn.edu/~wilf/AlgComp.pdf), Herbert S. Wilf
+ [Graph Theory](https://diestel-graph-theory.com/basic.html), Reinhard Diestel
+ [A Computational Introduction to Number Theory and Algebra](https://www.shoup.net/ntb/ntb-v2_1.pdf), Victor Shoup
+ [Abstract Algebra: Theory and Applications](http://abstract.ups.edu/download/aata-20130816.pdf), Thomas W. Judson
+ [Topology Without Tears](https://www.topologywithouttears.net/topbook.pdf), Sidney A. Morris
+ [A Concise Course in Algebraic Topology](https://www.math.uchicago.edu/~may/CONCISE/ConciseRevised.pdf), J. P. May
+ [Basic Category Theory](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1612.09375), Tom Leinster
+ [Classical Mechanics](https://www.physics.rutgers.edu/~shapiro/507/book.pdf), Joel A. Shapiro
+ [Handbook of Mathematical Functions](https://personal.math.ubc.ca/~cbm/aands/abramowitz_and_stegun.pdf), Milton Abramowitz, Irene A. Stegun
